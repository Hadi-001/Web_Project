🌿 Find Your Calm

Feeling anxious? Take a moment to slow down and breathe.

🧘 Grounding Anxiety Exercise

Try the 5-4-3-2-1 technique: Name 5 things you can see, 4 you can touch, 3 you can hear, 2 you can smell, and 1 you can taste.

“You don’t have to control your thoughts. You just have to stop letting them control you.” – Dan Millman

💡 Anxiety Management Tips

  • 🌬️ Practice deep breathing exercises.
  • 📖 Write down your worries to clear your mind.
  • 🏃 Engage in light exercise like stretching or yoga.
  • ☕ Limit caffeine intake, as it can increase anxiety.
  • 🎨 Engage in a creative activity like drawing or journaling.
  • 📞 Reach out to a friend or loved one for support.
